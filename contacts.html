Convenient

location

The area around Hotel Asia is conveniently located within walking distance of hospitals, convenience stores, and banks. It is also unique to the hotel that reliable taxis are always waiting at the door.

  • Hospital (5-minute walk from Cebu Doctor's Hospital, 7-minute walk from Cheongwa Hospital)
  • Japanese Help Desk (Cebu Doctor's Hospital)
  • Convenience store (4 minutes walk from Seven-Eleven)
  • Coffee shop (Bo's Coffee 2 minutes walk)
  • Each major bank branch (4 to 8 minutes on foot)
  • Supermarket (Robinson's 10 minutes walk)
  • Shopping mall (Ayala Center 10~15 minutes by taxi, SM City taxi 15~20 minutes)
