About Us

Our Tradition Of Best Hospitality

Two Decades of Warm Welcomes... In order to launch Hotel Asia, we relocated to Cebu from our native Japan. We are a family-owned and operated company. Through work-study, scholarships, and hospitality industry experience, we support talented Cebuanos locally. With the utmost humility and respect, we strive to deliver the finest service we can.

We offer affordable studios to luxurious suites. Han-nya, our Japanese restaurant, serves authentic cuisine year-round. Enjoy Japanese drinks and reserve private rooms. Our event space hosts large gatherings like meetings and weddings, seating hundreds when combined.

Greetings from the Founder

" First of all, I would like to express my deepest gratitude to the people of the Philippines. This hotel was built and operated with the help of these people. The fact that my wife is from Cebu is a big part of the construction of the hotel, but I don't know if Mr./Ms. know that the people of this country are famous in the world for being "accommodating people," "kind, unobtrusive" and "cheerful" people. They speak English, have a relatively high level of education, and are a place of beauty. I wondered if there was anything I could invest in such a nation, and I thought about what makes my life worthwhile, and that became the hotel.

Chairman of the Board Kunio Watanabe

Since it is built, it must be a convincing hotel. We placed great emphasis on quality, so during construction, I was often asked coldly, "Mr. Watanabe is going to build a five- or six-star hotel," and I replied that I would build a small, international-level hotel. It's not perfect, but I'm confident that it's convincing. In particular, we pay attention to the construction that can maintain cleanliness and quality, and we intend to maintain it.

Chairman of the Board Kunio Watanabe

It took 1 year and 11 months to build, and it opened in March 1999. The hotel is committed to safe, free and comfortable. On the 1st floor, there is a Japan dish "Hannya". The meaning is "Buddha's wisdom". It is said to be the ultimate wisdom. Cooking is the fruit of hundreds and thousands of years of wisdom. If it doesn't taste good, it's meaningless. There is a "bar" on the 8th floor, and it is an excellent location with a view of Cebu city.

Chairman of the Board Kunio Watanabe

We look forward to your cooperation in hotels, restaurants, and bars, as we will devote ourselves to further development. Finally, I would like to thank our friends in Japan for their strong support of us.."

Chairman of the Board Kunio Watanabe
