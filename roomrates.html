Main facilities common to all rooms

  • Wifi can be used 24 hours a day free of charge throughout the building (optical cable)
  • Toilet with washlet. Water also flows in the same way as in Japan.
  • Air conditioner (quiet type)
  • 21-inch TV (cable TV). There are 75 channels to watch NHK in Japanese.
  • Hair dryer
  • Refrigerator
  • Safety deposit box
  • You can make calls directly from your room, both domestically and internationally.
  • Hot water can be used in the bathroom. Deluxe rooms and above are stone baths.
